Frequently Asked Questions

Is my resume stored anywhere?

No. Your resume file is processed in-browser and sent directly to the Gemini API. It is never stored on our servers. Analysis results are only saved to Firestore if you explicitly click 'Save Analysis'.

How accurate is the ATS score?

The score is an AI estimate of keyword alignment and contextual fit — not a real ATS system output. Different employers use different ATS software. Use the score as a directional guide, not an absolute number.

What domains are supported?

AI/ML Engineer, Data Scientist, Software Developer, Data Engineer, DevOps Engineer, Full Stack Developer — and a custom option for any other role.

Do I need to create an account?

No. ResumeIQ is fully anonymous. No sign-up, no login, no email required.
